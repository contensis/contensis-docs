The `flip` parameter enables an image to be flipped on the `horizontal` or `vertical` axis, or on `both` axes.

---

## Flip image on horizontal axis

-   **Standard**
    
    [tree-frog.jpg?flip=horizontal](https://www.contensis.com/image-library/resources-images/image-api-examples/tree-frog.jpg?width=500&flip=horizontal)
    
-   **Short**
    
    [tree-frog.jpg?flip=h](https://www.contensis.com/image-library/resources-images/image-api-examples/tree-frog.jpg?width=500&flip=h)
    

![](https://www.contensis.com/image-library/resources-images/image-api-examples/tree-frog.jpg?width=500&flip=h)
