To easily convert an image to grayscale you can use the `effect` or `e` parameter with a value of `grayscale`.

---

-   **Standard**
    
    [tree-frog.jpg?effect=grayscale](https://www.contensis.com/image-library/resources-images/image-api-examples/tree-frog.jpg?effect=grayscale)
    
-   **Short**
    
    [tree-frog.jpg?e=grayscale](https://www.contensis.com/image-library/resources-images/image-api-examples/tree-frog.jpg?e=grayscale)
