The `blur` parameter applies a gaussian style blur to your image, smoothing out any image noise or to reduce the complexity of the image. It accepts values in the range of 1-100.

---

## Blur by 3

-   **Standard**
    
    [tree-frog.jpg?blur=3](https://www.contensis.com/image-library/resources-images/image-api-examples/tree-frog.jpg?blur=3)
    
-   **Short**
    
    [tree-frog.jpg?b=3](https://www.contensis.com/image-library/resources-images/image-api-examples/tree-frog.jpg?b=3)
    

![](https://www.contensis.com/image-library/resources-images/image-api-examples/tree-frog.jpg?width=500&b=3)
