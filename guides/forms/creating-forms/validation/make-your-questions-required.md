You can boost your response rates by allowing participants to skip questions they prefer not to answer. However, for crucial information, you can set specific questions as required to ensure you get the essential data you need.

## To mark a field as required in a form

1.  Locate and open the form you want to edit from the *Forms listing screen*.
2.  With the form opened for editing, select the field that you want to be mandatory.
3.  Select **Validation** from the field settings panel and turn on the **Required** toggle. You can optionally set a custom validation message that will be displayed if the user does not populate the field.
4.  **Save** and **Publish** your form.
