If you want to change the order of the fields displayed to your users in a form, you can do so in the form builder.

## Reorder fields using drag and drop

The easiest way to reorder the fields in a form is to use drag and drop.

1.  Click and drag the field that you want to reorder.
2.  Move the field up or down the list to its new location. A dashed line will be displayed to indicate where the field will be positioned once dropped.
3.  Drop the field into its new position.
4.  Click **Save** and **Publish** to ensure your users see the changes in the form.

## Reorder fields using the keyboard

You can also reorder the fields in the field list using the keyboard. When the field is selected, use the ALT+Up or ALT+Down arrow key combinations to move them up and down in the list.
