Including instructions on form fields guides users on what to enter, reducing errors and improving the user experience. They help users complete forms accurately and efficiently.

## Setting field instructions

1.  With a form opened for editing, select the field that you want to add instructions to.
2.  From the *Settings* panel locate the *Field instructions* property.
3.  Enter the instructions thatâ€™s relevant to the majority of users, like how their information will be used, or where to find it.
4.  Press **Save** and preview the changes to your form, and **Publish** when you are happy.

## Tips

-   Keep the text short and concise, the instructions text will be read by screen readers alongside the form labels.
-   Avoid using links in instructions text, we want to encourage visitors to complete the form, not navigate away.
