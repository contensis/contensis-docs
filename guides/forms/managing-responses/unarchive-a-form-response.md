Moving a form response out of the archive makes it available for reuse. Once unarchived it will be shown in the form response listing. You'll need to be a member of a role with the *Manage archive* permission for form responses to unarchive a response.

## Unarchive a form response from the response viewer

1.  Press the **Forms** button in the sidebar, then select **Forms responses** from the drawer menu.
2.  Choose a form to view its responses.
3.  Locate archived responses by toggling the **Show archived** option in the filter panel. Archived responses will be displayed in the listing.
4.  Open the response in the viewer.
5.  Press the **Actions** button, as indicated by the triple dot icon. A dropdown menu will be displayed.
6.  Select **Unarchive** from the menu. An *Unarchive confirmation* window will appear.
7.  Press the **Unarchive** button to confirm. A notification will be displayed confirming that the response has been unarchived. The response will be displayed in the form response listing screen.
