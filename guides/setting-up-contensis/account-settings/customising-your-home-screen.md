To change the screen you first see when you log in to Contensis, update the Default view from your user profile:

1.  From the sidebar, click on your user avatar or initials.
2.  Select **User profile** from the context menu. Your profile will be displayed.
3.  Locate the Default view setting and choose from the following options in the dropdown.
    
    -   Dashboard
    -   Entries
    -   Site View
    
4.  **Save** you changes, the next time you login you will be taken to your default view.
