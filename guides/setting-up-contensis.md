To change your Contensis user account password, log in to your Contensis and perform the steps outlined below:

1.  From the sidebar, click on your user avatar or initials.
2.  Select **User profile** from the context menu. Your profile will be displayed.
3.  Press the **Change password** button. A change passsword window will be displayed
4.  Type in your current password.
5.  Type in a new password.
6.  Press **Update password.**
