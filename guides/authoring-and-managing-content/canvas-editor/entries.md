Entries allow you to display dynamic content within the flow of your writing. They are automatically updated in the Canvas whenever the entry itself is updated, ensuring your content stays current.

### Uses

-   Adding a product card in the middle of a blog post
-   Inserting a testimonial from your central repository of testimonials
-   Including accessible video content modelled as an entry

### Add an entry

Type `/entry` and press `Enter`. A window will appear where you can choose the entry you want to insert.

### Edit an entry

Once an entry is inserted:

-   Press the edit button on the block toolbar to open the entry in the flyout editor.
-   With the entry focused, press the swap button to switch it for another.

### Delete an entry

If you no longer need the entry:

-   Press Delete on the block toolbar.
-   Press Del or Backspace with the block focused to remove it.
-   Use the actions menu by pressing Command/Control + / and selecting Delete from the menu.
