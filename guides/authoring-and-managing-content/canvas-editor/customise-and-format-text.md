As a rich text editor, you expect to be able to format the text that you've written. The formatting options available in the editor will depend on the [Canvas configuration](/help-and-docs/guides/authoring-and-managing-content/canvas-editor/inserting-and-customising-the-canvas-editor).

## Formatting bar

Selecting text with the mouse or keyboard will display the formatting toolbar. From this toolbar you can choose a wide variety of formatting options.

-   Bold
-   Italic
-   Strikethrough
-   Highlight
-   Code
-   Underline
-   Subscript
-   Superscript
-   Variable
-   Keyboard
-   Clear formatting

## Keyboard and markdown shortcuts

You can use commonly used shortcuts such as `Command/Ctrl+B` , `Command/Ctrl+I` or markdown syntax `**bold** __italic__` to make text bold or italic. The editor supports a wide variety of [shortcuts](/help-and-docs/guides/authoring-and-managing-content/canvas-editor/keyboard-and-markdown-shortcuts-for-the-canvas-editor) so you never have to leave your keyboard.
