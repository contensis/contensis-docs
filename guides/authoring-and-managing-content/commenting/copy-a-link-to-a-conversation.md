Sharing a link to a conversation can aid collaboration. When you want to share a link to an existing conversation, you can use the *Copy link to conversation* button from the conversation action menu.

1.  Open an entry for editing or review.
2.  Press the **View/Add comment** button for the field you want to edit. The *Comment*s panel will be displayed.
3.  From the conversation actions menu, select the **Copy link to conversation** option in the menu. A link to the conversation will be copied to the clipboard.
4.  Share the link to the conversation through Slack, email or any other messaging service you want to use.
