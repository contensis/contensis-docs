## Content owner changes

When a content owner is updated or unassigned from an entry, both the new and previous owners are notified.

## Form response assignee

When a person is assigned or unassigned from a form response, both the new and previous assignees receive notifications.

## Turning off email notifications

Email notifications in Contensis primarily focus on events that require action. If you prefer to turn off notifications completely then this can be done in your user profile.

1.  Press on your avatar in the sidebar.
2.  Select **User profile** from the popup menu and your user profile will be displayed.
3.  Scroll down to *Email notifications* and turn the **Send notifications by email** toggle off, followed by pressing **Save**.
4.  Email notifications will no longer be sent.

### Notifications that override your preferences

There are some notifications that you will recieve regardless of your email notification preferences.

-   When you are added or removed as the content owner of an entry.
-   When you are assigned or unassigned as the person responsible for a form response.
-   When you are directly @mentioned in a comment.
-   When you are specifically chosen to be the approver of an entry.
