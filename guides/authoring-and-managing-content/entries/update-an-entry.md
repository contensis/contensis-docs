4.  Entries

If you want to update or make a change to an existing entry, follow these steps:

1.  Press the **Entries** button in the sidebar to open the *Entries* listing screen.
2.  Press the title of the entry you want to edit in the listing. The *Entry Editor* screen will open ready for you to make your updates.
3.  Press **Save** and **Submit** to send your content for approval, or **Publish** if the workflow allows you to publish the changes to the entry.

**Note:** Take a look at our [versioning](https://www.contensis.com/help-and-docs/user-guides/authoring/entries/entry-versioning "Entry versioning") and [workflow states](https://www.contensis.com/help-and-docs/user-guides/governance/workflow/workflow-states "Workflow states") articles for details of how Contensis handles entries at different stages of the authoring process.
