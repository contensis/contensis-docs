4.  Saved views

If you have views that are no longer relevant, this article explains how to delete them.

1.  With a view panel displayed in the *Entry listing* or *Advanced search* screen, locate a view you want to delete and press the **Actions** button, indicated by the triple dot icon. A dropdown menu will show.
2.  Select **Delete** from the menu. A *Delete view?* confirmation window will appear.
3.  Confirm the deletion of the view by pressing the **Delete** button.
4.  A notification will be displayed confirming the deletion of the view.

**Note**: The wording in the Delete view window may vary depending on the view type. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your deletion.
