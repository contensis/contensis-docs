The Site View screen is restricted to users that are a member of a role with a [Site View permission](https://www.contensis.com/help-and-docs/user-guides/content-modelling/site-view/site-view-permissions) applied. You can also provide administration access to the Site View screen by adding users to the *Site View Administrators* security group.

To open Site View, select the **Site View** button in the sidebar.
