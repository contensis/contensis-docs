If you want to change the order of the fields displayed to your authors in the entry editor, you can do so in the content type builder.

## Reorder fields using drag and drop

The easiest way to reorder the fields in a content type is to use drag and drop.

1.  Press and drag on the field that you want to reorder.
2.  Move the field up or down the list to its new location. A dashed line will be displayed to indicate where the field will be positioned once dropped.
3.  Drop the field into its new position.
4.  Press **Save** and **Publish** to ensure your authors see the changes in the entry editor.

## Reorder fields using the actions menu

1.  With the field in the content type selected, press on the more actions button to open the menu. Depending on the position of the field, you'll be provided with a series of options:
    
    -   **Move up/down** will move the position of the field by one place.
    -   **Move to the top/bottom** will move the field to either end of the list.
    
2.  Once the field has been moved, **Save** and **Publish** your changes.

## Reorder fields using the keyboard

You can also reorder the fields in the field list using the keyboard. When the field is selected used the `ALT+Up` or `ALT+Down` arrow key combinations to move them up and down in the list.
