Users can be assigned to a group individually or as part of a child group.

## Assign users

1.  Locate the group you want to assign users to.
2.  Press the **Actions** button in the listing. A context menu will be displayed.
3.  Select **Assign users** from the menu. An Assign users window will be displayed.
4.  Use the search box to locate the users you wish to add to the group.
5.  Once you've made your selection, press the **Add users** button. A notification will be displayed confirming that the users have been added.

## Assign child group

1.  Locate the group you want to assign users to.
2.  Press the **Actions** button in the listing. A context menu will be displayed.
3.  Select **Assign child group** from the menu. An Assign child window will be displayed.
4.  Use the search box to locate the groups you wish to add to the group.
5.  Once you've made your selection, press the **Add groups** button. A notification will be displayed confirming that the groups have been added.
